# Report Templates

Use these structures when generating team reports. Adapt based on data available from ~~CRM and ~~back office.

## Morning Standup Report

Generate this report each morning to prepare the team leader for the day.

### Structure

**1. Overnight Activity**
- New leads received since yesterday (count, source breakdown)
- Lead response times (any leads >5 min response?)
- Deals updated (status changes, new notes, documents uploaded)

**2. Today's Critical Items**
- Contract deadlines today (inspection, appraisal, financing, closing)
- Scheduled showings and appointments
- Tasks overdue or due today

**3. Agent Attention Needed**
- Agents with no logged activity in past 48 hours
- Agents with leads that have not been contacted
- Agents with upcoming deadlines and no recent deal activity

**4. Quick Numbers**
- Active pipeline count and value
- Pending closings this week
- New leads in last 24 hours

## Weekly Accountability Report

Generate for weekly team meetings or 1-on-1s. Compare each agent's activity against their weekly targets.

### Structure

**1. Team Snapshot**
- Total units under contract
- Total units closed this week
- New leads distributed this week
- Team pipeline value change (up/down from last week)

**2. Agent Scorecard (table)**

| Agent | Contacts | Appts Set | Showings | Offers | Listings Taken | Closings | Notes |
|-------|----------|-----------|----------|--------|---------------|----------|-------|
| ... | ... / goal | ... / goal | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

**3. Wins**
- Agents who hit or exceeded targets
- Notable deals closed
- Positive client feedback

**4. Coaching Priorities**
- Agents below target (who needs attention?)
- Specific skill gaps observed
- Recommended coaching focus for next week

**5. Pipeline Health**
- Deals at risk (identify by flag criteria)
- Expected closings next 30 days
- Pipeline coverage ratio

## Monthly Production Report

Comprehensive monthly summary for team leaders and brokerage leadership.

### Structure

**1. Executive Summary**
- Total closed units and volume
- GCI earned
- Comparison to monthly goal (% achieved)
- Comparison to same month last year

**2. Agent Production Ranking**

| Rank | Agent | Units | Volume | GCI | Avg Sale Price | DOM |
|------|-------|-------|--------|-----|----------------|-----|
| 1 | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

**3. Lead Performance**
- Leads received by source
- Conversion rate by source
- Revenue per lead by source
- Cost per acquisition by source (if spend data available)

**4. Transaction Analysis**
- Average days on market (listings)
- List-to-sale price ratio
- Average time from contract to close
- Fall-through rate and reasons

**5. Recruiting & Roster**
- Current agent count
- New hires this month
- Departures this month
- Recruiting pipeline status

**6. Goals for Next Month**
- Team production target
- Key initiatives or focus areas
- Agents on performance improvement plans
- Upcoming closings expected
